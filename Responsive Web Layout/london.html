About London

London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a historic and diverse city known for its rich culture, iconic landmarks, and global influence. With attractions like the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, the British Museum, and the Houses of Parliament, it blends centuries of history with modern innovation.
London is a major financial, political, and cultural hub, offering a vibrant arts scene, world-class theatre, and renowned shopping districts like Oxford Street and Covent Garden. The city's extensive public transportation system and diverse neighborhoods make it a dynamic and cosmopolitan destination for people from around the world.
